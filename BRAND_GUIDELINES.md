# STORY HOMES — BRAND GUIDELINES

## Brand Overview
We are a marketplace that bridges the gap between homeowners and investors.

Our company acquires properties and connects them with investors, creating opportunities for renovation, resale, or rental.

We provide tailored solutions to sellers, ensuring a fast and efficient process that meets their specific needs.

---

## Logo Usage

### Do:
- Use the light-colored logo on darker backgrounds

### Do Not:
- Change the opacity of the logo
- Add drop shadows
- Rotate the logo
- Outline the logo
- Change logo colors outside of brand palette
- Distort the logo in any way

## Typography

### Primary Typeface: Lora
- Use for headers and accents
- Variants: Regular, Medium, Bold
- Maintain consistency across all branded materials

### Secondary Typeface: Montserrat
- Use for body text and longer content
- Variants: Book, Medium, Bold
- Preferred for readability across digital platforms

---

## Icon Guidelines

- Icons must be single color only
- A single icon must not contain multiple colors
- Multiple icons may use different secondary colors to differentiate meaning
- Maintain strong contrast for visibility on all backgrounds
- Ensure legibility, especially on dark or colored backgrounds

---

## Design Principles

- Maintain high contrast for readability
- Keep visuals clean and modern
- Avoid unnecessary visual effects (shadows, distortions, overlays)
- Prioritize clarity and simplicity over decoration
